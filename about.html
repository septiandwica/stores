About Us

Setsail BizAccel, as the President University Business Accelerator, has evolved into more than just an accelerator. We have now become a store that offers various products, including President University merchandise, books, and much more. With this step, we aim to be the primary destination for students and the university community, providing easy access to quality products that support entrepreneurial spirit and academic development. The revenue from product sales will be used to support the entrepreneurship ecosystem on campus.

Any questions? Let us know in store at Setsail Bizaccel - Medical Science Center, Jababeka, North CIkarang, Indonesia.
